Our Community Projects

We are currently building our digital infrastructure to better serve our community. The mission of Our Community Projects is to promote and further educational equity, civic engagement, and community resilience. The Corporation advances information awareness and use in communities and groups that are underserved in the face of rapidly evolving technologies. Disaster preparedness education and resources, animal welfare activities, and other community-level programs designed to encourage and develop learning at all ages will be developed using accessible programming, mobile and digital assets, and community focused networks. Local communities and the regional public will benefit and contribute to our success.
